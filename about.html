OUR services

PHOTO BOOTH

Make you Event more colorful and fun with our PHOTO BOOTH. We provide you the BEST services and high quality HD pictures using the latest technology. Printouts will be given to you and your guest too. Our Booth just takes 6x6 sq. ft. space but will bring endless smiles and capture all fun & heart melting moments. We are professionals. MEMRIZ serves COPERATE EVENTS too. Book today and get a chance to book your package upto $99 per hour.

PHOTO EDITING
